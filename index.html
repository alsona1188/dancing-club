What dance classes we offer?

Tango

Tango was originally formulated in Buenos Aires. Tango is a dance with dramatic flair, using “stalking” and “sneaking” walking characteristics that separate it from the other ballroom dancing styles. It is a very sensual and visually exciting choice for a first wedding dance!

Bachata

Bachata is a style of dance that originated in the Dominican Republic. Generally, most of the dancer’s movement is in the lower body up to the hips, and the upper body moves much less.

Wedding Classes

Learn a few basics or a fully choreographed first dance routine–it’s up to you! We promise great results and lots of fun for our brides, grooms, family, or anyone!
